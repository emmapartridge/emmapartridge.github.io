Meet Our Team

Dr. Jenessa Winston, DVM, PhD, DACVIM

Dr. Jenessa Winston is a highly experienced veterinarian and board-certified internist with a passion for advancing veterinary medicine. She completed both her DVM and PhD and is a Diplomate of the American College of Veterinary Internal Medicine (DACVIM). She has extensive research experience in gastrointestinal and liver diseases.

Dr. Adam Rudinsky, DVM, MS, DACVIM

Dr. Adam Rudinsky is a board-certified veterinary internist specializing in small animal medicine. With a Master's degree and a strong interest in endocrinology, Dr. Rudinsky brings years of experience in clinical practice and academic research to our team.

Dr. Valerie Parker, DVM, DACVIM

Dr. Valerie Parker is a distinguished veterinary internist with expertise in small animal internal medicine. She is a Diplomate of the American College of Veterinary Internal Medicine (DACVIM) and focuses her research on improving diagnostic and treatment options for gastrointestinal diseases.

Dr. Megan Schreeg, DVM, PhD, DACVP

Dr. Megan Schreeg is a veterinary pathologist with a PhD and board certification in veterinary clinical pathology (DACVP). Her research focuses on the pathology of gastrointestinal diseases in companion animals and their translational relevance to human health.

John Rowe, DVM

John Rowe is a compassionate veterinarian with years of experience in small animal practice. He works closely with our research team to apply clinical knowledge to ongoing veterinary research projects.

Tomina Rahman

Tomina Rahman is a research associate who specializes in veterinary clinical research. She plays a pivotal role in managing data collection and analysis for our ongoing studies.

Regan Jennings

Regan Jennings is a dedicated research assistant working alongside Dr. Winston and our team to explore cutting-edge veterinary research, particularly focusing on gastrointestinal and hepatobiliary diseases in pets.

Kaitlyn Ingram

Kaitlyn Ingram is a passionate veterinary technician who provides valuable support in the lab and with clinical trials. She has a strong interest in improving animal health through research.

Molly Welton

Molly Welton is a veterinary assistant who brings a wealth of experience to our team. She supports the day-to-day operations and provides care to the animals involved in research studies.

Emma Partridge

Emma Partridge is the head of innovation at Ardent Animal Health, where she leads research efforts in regenerative medicine and advanced therapeutics. Emma is passionate about bridging the gap between veterinary care and cutting-edge biomedical research.
